Teacher Documentation

Welcome to the Teacher Dashboard!

This guide will walk you through the key features and functionalities available to you.

Creating an Exam

To create an exam, click on the "Create New Exam" button on your dashboard. You will need to provide the following information:

  • Title: The title of the exam.
  • Class: The class for which the exam is intended.
  • Duration: The duration of the exam in minutes.
  • Description: Instructions or any other relevant information for the students.

Adding Questions

After creating an exam, you can add questions in two ways:

  1. Manually: Click on the "Add Question" button to add questions one by one. You can choose from different question types, such as multiple-choice, single-choice, and short answer.
  2. Bulk Upload: You can upload a batch of questions using an Excel or CSV file. The file should have the following columns: `question_text`, `question_type`, `option1`, `option2`, `option3`, `option4`, and `correct_answer`.

Managing Exams

You can manage your exams from the dashboard. You can view the number of submissions, see the completion rate, and delete exams that are no longer needed.

Viewing Analytics

Click on the "Analytics" button to view detailed analytics for each exam. You can see the scores of each student, the average score, and the completion rate.
